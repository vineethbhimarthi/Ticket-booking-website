BOOK TRAIN TICKETS ACROSS INDIA

Getting the right train tickets the right price can be complicated these days, but that's where National Railway Tourism Corporation(NRTC) comes in.We use the latest technology to source the lowest fares for all kinds of train tickets and rail passes – often with extra discounts too, based on different time options and combinations of one way fares. Choose from an unrivalled range of States and destinations, and create your own unique rail experience.

How to Book Train Tickets:

It's easy to book train tickets with NRTC. Simply browse or search for the journey you want to make,and complete the simple online booking form. If you need help or advice at any stage, our friendly staff will be delighted to assist you from our call center.
